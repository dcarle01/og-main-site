"All right... It is Baratta."
"We are a non-physical being of light. We are not involved in a physical lifetime at this time. We have had a few lifetime experiences -- long, long ago. We now work in a different way with human beings, both telepathically and through this channeling process with the one known as Dale. And in this work we seek to touch people's hearts, to touch that wonderful shining light, that spark of love that is within their hearts ... to remind them of the beauty of that love, and of the beautiful desires, beautiful dreams that they hold close to that heart."

✨ Baratta's Purpose

Baratta seeks to assist people in reaching beyond the seeming limitations of their current lives, helping them claim their birthright: the dreams and desires they hold in their heart.

"We do at times talk to people about their past life experiences, if those experiences are relevant to the issue at hand. At other times, we talk to people about experiences they may have had within this lifetime that are still affecting their lives now. We seek to assist people in reaching beyond the seeming limitations of their current lives, of their current reality, and to claiming that which is their birthright: the dreams, the desires that they hold in their heart."

"We assist people in reaching for those dreams, in reaching for the love that those dreams represent, and in reaching for the expression of that love from within themselves. We seek to assist people in the discovery that the love they seek to create in their lives is already there as a potential, is already there as an innate part of them, and has only to be unfolded, and brought into the light of day."

🌟 Personal Guidance

Baratta helps individuals understand the underlying patterns shaping their current reality and offers tools for gentle transformation.

💫 Realization of Dreams

Assisting people in claiming their birthright - the beautiful dreams and desires they hold close to their hearts.

💖 Discovery of Love Within

Helping people discover that the love they seek is already within them as a potential, waiting to be unfolded.

🔮 Past Life Insights

When relevant to current issues, Baratta shares insights about past life experiences that may be affecting present circumstances.

🌈 Spiritual Intimacy

Creating a level of safety and gentleness where people feel known and respected at a profound level.
