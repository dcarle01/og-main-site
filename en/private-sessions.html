🌟 What awaits you in a session with Baratta?

It is a special time where you can talk with Baratta about any issue or question you would like to explore. At the start of the session, with your permission, he will take a few moments to look deeply into your consciousness before inviting your personal questions.

As you share your ideas and concerns with him, he gently moves his awareness closer to the issues at hand, and then gives you a clear view of the underlying currents and patterns that are shaping your current reality.

Baratta will offer suggestions, tools and techniques that can gently and effectively reshape those issues that are creating difficulty, and with the successes are flowing beautifully into your life, he will pause to celebrate them with you.

✨ A Unique Experience of Spiritual Intimacy

Within these sessions Baratta weaves a deep sense of spiritual intimacy, and creates a level of safety and gentleness that is quite unique. As a result, you will feel that you are known and respected at a level you may never have experienced before.

💻 Via Zoom or Telephone

Private sessions can be via Zoom or Telephone. Baratta is not limited by physical distance, and many clients have said that they can actually feel Baratta's presence with them during their session.

🎵 Recording Included

Your session with Baratta will be recorded for your personal use. The recording will be sent by secure download, at no extra cost, within three business days of your appointment.

🔒 Completely Confidential

All private sessions with Baratta are completely confidential. Your personal information and session content remain private and secure.
